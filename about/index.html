About

Melanie Cebula is an expert in Cloud Infrastructure, where she is recognized worldwide for explaining radically new ways of thinking about cloud efficiency and usability. Her knowledge is backed by real work on the ground; She has directly collaborated on over $75M+ (and counting...) in savings to her company's cloud data centers since 2019.

Besides her expertise in the computing world, Melanie is an active multi-instrumentalist and electronic producer; her first EP is coming out in 2025. Melanie is also passionate about yoga, rock climbing, community work & community building, costuming, foraging and crafting.
